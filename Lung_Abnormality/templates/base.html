Lung Diseases Information


Pneumonia

Infection that causes inflammation and fluid buildup in the lungs, affecting breathing.

Tuberculosis

Infection that affects the lungs,causing severe respiratory symptoms and damage.

Covid-19

Affect lungs, causing respiratory symptoms such as cough and difficulty in breathing.

Lung Cancer

Malignant tumor that originates in lungs, causing severe respiratory complications.
